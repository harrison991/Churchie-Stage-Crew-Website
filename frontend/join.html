Join Stage Crew

Ready to step into the world of technical theatre? Discover how you can become part of our dynamic Stage Crew community and start your journey in production excellence.

Why Join Stage Crew?

Stage Crew offers an incredible opportunity to develop technical skills, work as part of a dedicated team, and play a crucial role in bringing theatrical productions to life. Whether you're interested in lighting, sound, set construction, or video production, we have a place for you.

Hands-On Learning

Gain practical experience with professional-grade equipment and industry-standard techniques.

Teamwork & Leadership

Develop collaboration skills and take on leadership roles in a supportive environment.

Career Preparation

Build skills and connections that can lead to careers in theatre, film, events, and technology.

Creative Expression

Use technology and design to enhance storytelling and create memorable experiences.

Membership Requirements

Eligibility

  • Current ACGS student (Years 7-12)
  • Willingness to commit to training and productions
  • Positive attitude and team spirit
  • No prior experience required

Time Commitment

  • Minimum 4 hours per week training
  • Additional time during production weeks
  • Weekend workshops and events
  • Flexible scheduling around academics

Safety Requirements

  • Complete safety orientation training
  • Follow all equipment and workshop protocols
  • Wear appropriate safety gear when required
  • Maintain focus and attention during activities

Commitment

  • Respect for equipment and facilities
  • Support fellow crew members
  • Professional attitude during productions
  • Continuous learning and improvement

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any previous experience to join Stage Crew?

Not at all! Stage Crew welcomes students of all experience levels. Our comprehensive training program will teach you everything you need to know, starting with the basics and building up to advanced techniques. What matters most is enthusiasm and willingness to learn.

How much time do I need to commit each week?

The minimum commitment is about 4 hours per week for training sessions. During production weeks (typically 2-3 times per term), you may need to commit additional time for rehearsals and performances. We work around your academic schedule and other commitments.

Can I specialize in multiple areas?

Absolutely! Many of our members develop skills across multiple areas. We encourage cross-training as it makes you more versatile and gives you a better understanding of how all the technical elements work together. You can start with one area and expand your skills over time.

What if I have conflicts with other activities?

We understand that students have multiple commitments. Our training schedule is flexible, and we work with members to accommodate other activities. During your application interview, we'll discuss your schedule and find ways to make Stage Crew work for you.

Is there a cost to join Stage Crew?

Stage Crew membership is included in your co-curricular activities fee. There are no additional costs for training, equipment use, or participation in productions. We provide all necessary safety equipment and tools.

Can I join mid-term or do I have to wait for the start of the year?

You can join at any time during the year! We have rolling admissions and conduct information sessions monthly. New members receive catch-up training to bring them up to speed with current activities and safety requirements.
