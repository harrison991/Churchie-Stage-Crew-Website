About Stage Crew

Discover the passion, dedication, and technical expertise that drives our Stage Crew co-curricular program at Anglican Church Grammar School.

Our Mission

To provide students with hands-on experience in technical theatre, fostering creativity, teamwork, and professional skills that extend far beyond the stage. We believe in empowering young minds to bring artistic visions to life through technical excellence.

Our Vision

To be the premier technical theatre program in Brisbane, developing the next generation of creative professionals and instilling a lifelong appreciation for the arts and technical innovation.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all we do
  • Collaboration: Working together to achieve common goals
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and creative solutions
  • Respect: Valuing each team member's contribution
  • Growth: Continuously learning and developing our skills

Our History

2017

Foundation

Stage Crew was established as a formal co-curricular activity with just 15 enthusiastic students and basic equipment.

2018

First Major Production

Successfully managed technical aspects of the school's first major musical production, "Grease," showcasing our growing capabilities.

2020

Digital Innovation

Adapted to virtual productions during COVID-19, pioneering livestream technologies and digital stage design.

2022

Equipment Upgrade

Invested in state-of-the-art lighting systems, digital mixing consoles, and professional-grade video equipment.

2025

Present Day

Now boasting 150+ active members and supporting 25+ productions annually, we continue to grow and innovate.
