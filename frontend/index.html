What We Do

Lighting Design

Master the art of theatrical lighting, from basic spotlights to complex moving light systems and LED technology.

Sound Engineering

Learn professional audio mixing, microphone techniques, and sound system operation for live performances.

Set Construction

Build and design stage sets, props, and backdrops that bring theatrical visions to reality.

Video Production

Operate cameras, edit footage, and create multimedia content for school productions and events.
