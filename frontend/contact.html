Visit Our Facilities

Our State-of-the-Art Facilities

Stage Crew operates from multiple purpose-built facilities across the ACGS campus, each designed to provide students with professional-grade training environments.

Main Theatre

400-seat auditorium with professional lighting rig, sound system, and projection capabilities.

Workshop Facility

Fully equipped woodworking and metalworking shop for set construction and prop building.

Audio Control Room

Professional-grade mixing console and recording equipment for sound engineering training.

Media Production Suite

Video editing stations, camera equipment, and live streaming setup for multimedia projects.

Costume & Design Room

Dedicated space for costume creation, makeup application, and design work.

Equipment Storage

Climate-controlled storage for lighting equipment, sound gear, and production materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to get information about joining Stage Crew?

The best way is to attend one of our monthly information sessions or contact Mr. Mitchell directly via email. We also encourage prospective members to visit our facilities during training times to see Stage Crew in action.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during the school term. During school holidays, responses may take 2-3 business days. For urgent matters, please call the main school number.

Can parents/guardians visit the facilities?

Absolutely! We welcome parent visits and offer regular open days. Parents can schedule tours to see our facilities and meet our staff. We believe parent involvement and understanding is important for student success.

Do you offer technical support for other school events?

Yes! Stage Crew provides technical support for many school events beyond drama productions, including assemblies, concerts, sports presentations, and special ceremonies. Contact us to discuss your event's technical needs.

How can external organizations partner with Stage Crew?

We're always interested in partnership opportunities that benefit our students' learning. This includes guest workshops, equipment demonstrations, work experience placements, and collaborative projects. Please reach out to discuss potential partnerships.
