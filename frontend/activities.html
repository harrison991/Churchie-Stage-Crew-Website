Our Activities

Explore the diverse range of technical theatre activities, training programs, and hands-on experiences available in our Stage Crew program.

Core Training Areas

Lighting Design & Operation

Beginner Friendly

Learn the fundamentals of stage lighting, from basic color theory to advanced moving light programming.

  • LED and conventional lighting systems
  • Lighting console programming
  • Color theory and mood creation
  • Follow spot operation
  • Rigging and safety protocols
Tuesdays & Thursdays, 3:30-5:30 PM

Sound Engineering

Intermediate

Master the art of live sound mixing, microphone techniques, and audio system design.

  • Digital mixing console operation
  • Microphone placement and types
  • Monitor mixing and feedback control
  • Recording and playback systems
  • Live streaming audio setup
Wednesdays & Fridays, 3:30-5:30 PM

Set Construction & Design

Beginner Friendly

Build impressive stage sets, props, and backdrops using traditional and modern construction techniques.

  • Carpentry and woodworking
  • Scenic painting techniques
  • Prop construction and design
  • 3D printing for props
  • Stage mechanics and automation
Mondays & Saturdays, 2:00-5:00 PM

Video Production

Advanced

Create professional video content, operate cameras, and manage live streaming for events.

  • Multi-camera video production
  • Live streaming and recording
  • Video editing and post-production
  • Motion graphics and effects
  • Projection mapping
Thursdays & Sundays, 1:00-4:00 PM

Stage Management

Intermediate

Learn the coordination and management skills essential for running smooth productions.

  • Production scheduling and planning
  • Cue calling and show running
  • Safety management protocols
  • Team coordination and communication
  • Emergency procedures
All production days as needed

Costume & Makeup Design

Beginner Friendly

Design and create costumes, special effects makeup, and character transformations.

  • Costume design and construction
  • Special effects makeup
  • Period costume research
  • Fabric selection and manipulation
  • Character development through design
Tuesdays & Saturdays, 1:00-4:00 PM

Special Events & Workshops

Masterclass Series

Monthly workshops led by industry professionals covering advanced techniques, new technologies, and career pathways in technical theatre.

  • Guest lighting designers from major theatres
  • Sound engineers from recording studios
  • Film and TV production professionals
  • Theatre company technical directors

Annual Tech Olympics

A fun competition between teams testing skills across all technical areas, fostering teamwork and celebrating expertise.

  • Speed lighting design challenges
  • Sound mixing competitions
  • Rapid set construction tasks
  • Technical troubleshooting scenarios

Industry Partnerships

Collaborations with local theatres, production companies, and entertainment venues providing real-world experience.

  • QPAC education programs
  • Local theatre company internships
  • Event production company work experience
  • University technical theatre programs

Exchange Programs

Opportunities to work with other schools and participate in interstate theatre festivals and technical conferences.

  • Interstate school exchanges
  • National theatre festivals
  • Technical theatre conferences
  • International school partnerships

Skill Progression Pathway

Beginner

Foundation Skills

Basic safety training, equipment familiarization, and fundamental techniques in chosen specialization areas.

  • Safety certification
  • Basic equipment operation
  • Team collaboration skills
  • Show etiquette and professionalism
Intermediate

Specialized Training

Advanced techniques in 1-2 specialization areas, leadership opportunities, and cross-training in multiple disciplines.

  • Advanced technical skills
  • Mentoring newer members
  • Production assistant roles
  • Creative input and design work
Advanced

Leadership & Innovation

Leading productions, teaching others, exploring cutting-edge technologies, and preparing for industry careers.

  • Production leadership roles
  • Training and workshops delivery
  • Industry project collaboration
  • Career pathway planning
